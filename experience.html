Internship at AgileRadioCom

Role: Software Engineering Intern

Focus Area: TSN (Time-Sensitive Networking) Schedulers

Logo from agileradiocom.com

Overview

During my internship at AgileRadioCom, I worked on developing and optimizing Time-Sensitive Networking (TSN) schedulers, which are critical for ensuring deterministic communication in industrial and automotive networks.

Key Responsibilities

  • Developed scheduling algorithms for time critical data transmission in TSN networks
  • Implemented and tested various scheduling policies to optimize network performance
  • Analyzed network traffic patterns and latency requirements for different use cases
  • Collaborated with the engineering team to integrate schedulers into the existing network stack
  • Conducted performance testing and validation of scheduler implementations

Technologies Used

Programming Languages
Python
Networking Protocols
IEEE 802.1Qbv (Time-Aware Shaping), IEEE 802.1AS (Timing and Synchronization)
Tools
Wireshark, Linux, Github
Concepts
Real time systems, network scheduling, Quality of Service (QoS), deterministic networking

What I Learned

This internship gave me valuable hands on experience with cutting edge networking technology and real time systems. I learned how to balance competing requirements in resource constrained environments and gained a deeper understanding of how modern industrial networks operate. Working with TSN schedulers taught me the importance of precise timing and coordination in distributed systems.

The experience also strengthened my skills in algorithm development, system level programming, and collaborative software development in a professional engineering environment.
